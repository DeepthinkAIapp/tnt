Materials We Handle

Our concrete dumpsters are designed to handle a variety of heavy construction and demolition materials. We ensure proper disposal while maintaining safety and compliance standards.

Concrete

Broken concrete, slabs, foundations, and concrete debris from construction projects.

Weight: Heavy - up to 4,000 lbs per cubic yard

Brick & Masonry

Bricks, blocks, stone, and other masonry materials from demolition or renovation.

Weight: Heavy - up to 3,000 lbs per cubic yard

Asphalt

Asphalt paving, driveways, and road materials from resurfacing projects.

Weight: Heavy - up to 3,500 lbs per cubic yard

Construction Debris

Mixed construction materials including concrete, brick, and other heavy debris.

Weight: Varies - typically 2,000-4,000 lbs per cubic yard
